About METATONEHEN

Our cosmic journey and mission

A Cosmic Portal for Transformation

METATONEHEN was born from a vision to create a sacred space where cosmic wisdom and modern spiritual practices converge. Our name is accompanied by the Greek phrase "μετὰ τὸ νέἕν" which carries profound spiritual meaning.

The Meaning of METATONEHEN

The Greek phrase can be understood through its components:

  • μετὰ (meta): "After," "beyond," or "with," depending on context.
  • τὸ (to): "The" (definite article).
  • νέἕν (nehen): A variation suggesting "new one," similar to "neon" in Greek.

This phrase can be interpreted as "Beyond the New One" or "With the New One," suggesting a philosophical or spiritual transcendence of a novel state of unity. It symbolizes a journey or understanding that surpasses what is new and reaches something even greater.

We believe that each person carries a unique cosmic blueprint that, when understood and honored, leads to authentic living and spiritual evolution. Through sacred geometry, astrology, Human Design, and other modalities, we guide seekers on their journey of self-discovery and transformation.
